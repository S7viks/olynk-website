## Brand Identity

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- `#2B5288` - Deep Blue (Primary brand color, trust, intelligence)
- `#223148` - Dark Navy (Headers, authority, depth)
- `#D2C7B8` - Warm Beige (Accents, approachability)

**Secondary Colors:**
- `#F5E0D9` - Soft Peach (Backgrounds, human-centered)
- `#F3EAE0` - Cream (Section backgrounds, clarity)
- `#2F486D` - Steel Blue (Professional, reliable)

**Functional Colors:**
- Success: `#10B981` (Green)
- Warning: `#F59E0B` (Amber)
- Error: `#EF4444` (Red)
- Neutral: `#FFFFFF` (White), `#1F2937` (Charcoal)

### Typography
- **Font Family:** Inter (Primary), JetBrains Mono (Code/Data)
- **Headings:** Inter Bold, 48-72px
- **Body:** Inter Regular, 18-20px
- **Numbers/Stats:** Inter Bold, 64px
- **Line Height:** 1.6 for body, 1.2 for headings

### Visual Style
- Dark backgrounds with white text for key sections
- Generous white space (padding: 80-120px vertical)
- Subtle animations (scroll-triggered, < 300ms)
- Clean, minimal interface elements
- No stock photos - authentic dashboards and data visualizations

## Copy & Content

### Voice & Tone
- **Professional but approachable**
- **Confident but transparent**
- **Data-driven but human**
- **Technical but accessible**

### Writing Guidelines
- Short sentences (< 20 words)
- Active voice
- Numbers over words ("87.4%" not "high accuracy")
- Concrete benefits ("Save â‚¹2-8L monthly" not "reduce costs")
- No jargon unless necessary

### Content Hierarchy
1. **Problem** (emotional hook)
2. **Credibility** (build trust)
3. **Solution** (show capability)
4. **Proof** (testimonials + metrics)
5. **CTA** (remove risk, create urgency)

## Conversion Strategy

### Primary Conversion Goal
**Demo Request** - "Book a Demo" button

### Secondary Conversions
- "Talk to the AI" (interactive demo)
- Newsletter signup
- Resource downloads (whitepapers, guides)

### Conversion Funnel
1. **Homepage visit** (awareness)
2. **Scroll engagement** (interest)
3. **Feature exploration** (consideration)
4. **Demo request** (intent)
5. **Demo call** (evaluation)

### Optimization Tactics
- Multiple CTAs throughout page
- Risk-free guarantee (60-day money-back)
- Social proof (testimonials + logos)
- Urgency ("Setup in 24 hours")
- Trust indicators (SOC2, GDPR badges)

### A/B Test Ideas

- CTA button text ("Book Demo" vs "Get Started")
- Testimonial format (carousel vs grid)

- Problem section order
