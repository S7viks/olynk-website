**Version:** 1.0

## Executive Summary

Analyzed 16 leading AI, operations, and data platforms to identify best practices for OLynk's website. Key findings:

### Top Patterns
1. **Ultra-minimal heroes** (5-8 words max)
2. **"How It Works" in 3 steps** with icons/visuals
3. **Interactive demos** prominently featured
4. **Multiple CTA options** (Try Free + Book Demo)
5. **Client logos above the fold**
6. **Product videos** (30-90 seconds)
7. **Use cases/templates** over abstract features
8. **Community elements** (builds, testimonials, success stories)
