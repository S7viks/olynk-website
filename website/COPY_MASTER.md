**Feature Description:**
"Auto-create purchase orders when stockout risk exceeds 80%"

**Jargon-heavy:**
"Synergistic multi-modal intelligence infrastructure"

## Writing Checklist

Before publishing any copy, verify:

- [ ] Is it specific? (Numbers, examples, concrete outcomes)
- [ ] Is it credible? (Realistic metrics, not hype)
- [ ] Is it clear? (No jargon, 8th-grade reading level)
- [ ] Is it concise? (< 20 words per sentence)
- [ ] Is it actionable? (Clear next step)
- [ ] Is it human? (No robot-speak)
- [ ] Is it benefit-focused? (Not feature-focused)
